Day 1:

Tuesday 12th March 2024

Now, you're probably wondering why I've started this diary on the 12th, and not the 10th.
This is because I've been away for the weekend, where there was a lot of drinking and sinful moments.
I figured it would be unfair to myself and rude if I made a mockery of this beautiful religion.
And that is why I've started the fasting practice on this day, I wanted to start off fresh.
I won't be praying or anything like that, as I feel like I ain't there yet.
So until then, it's just fasting.
I'm going to try and log my daily experience as much as I can, I'll be logging in the times for both suhur and iftar,
what I've eaten and how I'm feeling throughout the day.
I don't know if I can make the full month, but I'll try my best and take it day by day, and hopefully accomplish some sort of spiritual discipline.
